Past Projects:
  • Project FixIT

    • Capstone Project that modernized the Sponsor's business processes with cloud-based solutions that resulted in significant changes. The implemented solutions streamlined internal workflows, tracking logistics, entity storage, asset management, and inventory invoicing.

  • Technical Intelligence Solutions (TIS)

    • Capstone Project that sought to provide a Small Disadvantaged Business with asset management, training management, and Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) compliance. The capstone team was successful in the integration of robust tracking systems that favor cloud-based Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solutions with improved role-based access controls, able to visualize metrics, and automate all task processes.
