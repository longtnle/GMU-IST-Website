Information Sciences and Technology Capstone General Information:


Welcome to George Mason University’s Information Sciences and Technology Capstone Program. The Capstone is designed to give undergraduate students the opportunity to work with their local area communities by solving real world business problems for organizations in need of Information Technology solutions. The Capstone serves as the culminating point for Information Technology students to demonstrate proper application of the technical skills and business concepts learned throughout their degree’s curriculum.

Designing and Implementing information technology solutions in a real-world setting is an empowering experience for students as they collaborate with client sponsors to fulfill meaningful projects in a professional setting. Over the course of two semesters, students will work in a team environment and apply the necessary skills to ensure successful completion and delivery of business requirements for projects. Students are expected to conduct strong professional communication with their sponsors and to solve real-world challenges with robust solutions

Students studying for a bachelor’s degree in Information Technology must complete their capstone course before graduation.
